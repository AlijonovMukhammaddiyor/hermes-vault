# PD101 — Behavioral

> Walk into a senior/staff behavioral loop with 8–10 true, polished STAR-L stories from real Rizo GO work, delivered with structure and credibility and adapted live to any prompt.

**Credits:** 2  ·  **Domain:** behavioral-interview  ·  **Prereqs:** none

## Enduring understandings
- Selection beats memorization — the right story for the prompt wins over a rehearsed script.
- Precise numbers feel real; round numbers feel invented. Evidence at the right level is the signal.
- Seniority shows in direction-setting, ambiguity, and influence — not in tasks completed.

## Grading policy
hw 40% · finals 60%

## Weekly schedule, outcomes & readings

### Sem 1 · Week 1 · Story Mining — extract 8–10 candidate stories from real Rizo GO work
- **create** — Mine 8–10 candidate stories from real Rizo GO experience across the senior dimensions (ownership, ambiguity, conflict, impact, scale).
  - *proof:* A story bank of 8-10 candidate stories mapped to senior dimensions, each with the hard part + evidence

### Sem 1 · Week 2 · STAR Drafting — shape stories into STAR-L with quantified impact
- **create** — Shape a mined story into STAR-L with quantified impact and first-person ownership.
  - *proof:* A story shaped as STAR-L with quantified impact + first-person ownership + a Learning

### Sem 1 · Week 3 · Semester 1 Checkpoint (delivery + self-critique)
- **evaluate** — Deliver a story to an unseen prompt, scored on the 5 dimensions, and root-cause the weakest dimension.
  - *proof:* Deliver a story to an unseen prompt, scored on all 5 dims, + a root-cause self-critique of the weakest dim

## Assessment plan
- **a.mine.bank** (summative, project, ≥create) — A story bank of 8-10 candidate stories mapped to senior dimensions, each with the hard part + evidence
- **a.star.draft** (summative, project, ≥create) — A story shaped as STAR-L with quantified impact + first-person ownership + a Learning
- **a.s1.final** (summative, explain, ≥evaluate) — Deliver a story to an unseen prompt, scored on all 5 dims, + a root-cause self-critique of the weakest dim
