# PD101 — Behavioral (2 credits)  ·  activates W9

## Outcomes
Walk into a FAANG-senior behavioral loop with 8–10 polished, true STAR-L stories mined from real
Rizo GO work, delivered with structure and credibility, and adapted live to any prompt.

## Units (unlock in order)
1. Story mining — extract 8–10 candidate stories from real Rizo GO experience (leadership,
   conflict, failure, ambiguity, impact, scale).
2. STAR drafting — shape each into Situation · Task · Action · Result · **Learning**; quantify impact.
3. Mock delivery — deliver stories to prompts; timing, structure, ownership ("I" not "we").
4. Rapid-fire — map one story to many prompts; handle follow-ups and curveballs.

## Grade weights
Story bank 40 · Mocks 60.

## Rubric (5 × 3 pt), outcome-calibrated
- Substance — is the story a real, senior-level example with genuine stakes?
- Structure — clean STAR-L, no rambling, clear arc.
- Relevance — actually answers the prompt asked.
- Credibility — specific, owned ("I did"), quantified, honest.
- Differentiation — memorable, shows senior judgment, not generic.

## Proof of work
STAR-L stories written to `Concepts/behavioral/<story>.md`; mocks logged with 5-dim scores +
root-cause fixes for weak dimensions. Recalibrate the bar against real interview outcomes.
